## Novel View Quality Testing (~08:00-08:45 UTC)

### 3DGS Novel View Tolerance
Tested renders at various offsets from training camera pose 15:

| Movement | 5cm | 10cm | 15cm | 20cm | 30cm | 50cm |
|----------|-----|------|------|------|------|------|
| Side | OK | Degraded | Bad | — | Worse | Unusable |
| Forward | — | Moderate | — | Bad | — | — |
| Height | Degraded | Bad | — | — | — | — |
| Yaw (°) | — | 10°=Bad | — | 20°=Worse | — | — |

**Conclusion:** 3DGS from 30 images supports ±5cm / ±5° novel views max. Beyond that, significant artifacts (smeary, blobby).

### Diffusion Inpainting Experiments
Tested multiple approaches to "fix" degraded novel views:

1. **SD Inpainting** (stabilityai model)
   - Mask from pixel diff to reference
   - Result: Clean kitchen but **totally hallucinated** — wrong floor, wrong cabinets

2. **SD img2img** (runwayml/stable-diffusion-v1-5)
   - Various strength values (0.3, 0.5, 0.7)
   - Low strength: Preserved smeary structure, looked painterly
   - High strength: Clean but hallucinated different kitchen

3. **ControlNet + MiDaS depth** (lllyasviel/control_v11f1p_sd15_depth)
   - Depth from MiDaS DPT_Large
   - Result: Preserved geometry (cabinet position, floor plane) but **wrong colors/textures**

4. **IP-Adapter + ControlNet**
   - Used GT reference image for style conditioning
   - ip_scale=0.8, cn_scale=0.3
   - Result: Right color palette, hexagonal-ish floor, but still distorted/wrong details

**Fundamental Problem:** Diffusion models generate *plausible* kitchens, not *this* kitchen. For robotics ground truth, this approach fails.
